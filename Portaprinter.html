Introducing the
PortaPrinter

Portable

With the dimensions 40x32x 5.8 and a print volume of 20x20x20, the PortaPrinter is roughly the size of a laptop, easy to transport from home, to your workspace, and to wherever else is necessary.

Wireless

The PortaPrinter is controlled through a computer or mobile app containing simple and advanced settings for users with different levels of experience.

3D printed 3D Printer

Made out of 3D printable custom parts, the PortaPrinter can be easily fixed, good as new.

Accessorize

The PortaPrinter has en ecosystem of accessories that enhances its capabilities.
