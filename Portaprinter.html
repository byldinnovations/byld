Introducing the
PortaPrinter

Portable

With the dimensions 40x32x5.8 and a print volume of 18x18x20, the PortaPrinter is roughly the size of a laptop, easy to transport from home, to your workspace, and to wherever else is necessary.

Wireless

The PortaPrinter is controlled through the PortaConnect app containing simple and advanced settings for users with different levels of experience.

Cost Effective

With the start price of $200, the PortaPrinter delivers innovation without breaking the bank

Accessorize

The PortaPrinter has en ecosystem of accessories that enhances its capabilities.
